My Story into Tech

2015 - 2018: The Foundation

Bachelor of Commerce & Computers

Started my journey at Osmania University, blending business logic with computer science fundamentals. This dual perspective taught me that great code isn't just about syntax; it's about solving real business problems efficiently.

2018 - 2019: The Builder Phase

Full Stack Developer at Quadrant Resources

Dove headfirst into enterprise development. I spent these years building tangible tools from scratch—event management portals and internal systems using .NET MVC, React, and SQL. I learned how to architect end-to-end solutions that people actually use.

2019 - 2022: Scaling to the Cloud

Software Developer at Azure Cosmos DB

Transitioned into Microsoft to work on globally distributed databases. Here, the scale shifted drastically. I moved from building apps to engineering the underlying cloud infrastructure, automating CI/CD pipelines, and writing telemetry to monitor millions in Azure spend.

2023 - Present: Ensuring Reliability

SRE – Azure SQL DB Capacity Management

Today, I operate at massive scale. I drive the modernization of entire fleets, decommissioning legacy hardware, and integrating Operational AI to catch incidents before they happen. My focus is on turning reactive operational chaos into proactive, automated stability.
